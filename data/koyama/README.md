

# Title of Dataset: Great Plains grassland methane dynamics
---

CH<sub>4</sub> flux data and gas diffusivity were collected ca. bi-weekly from 22 filed plots in seven sites across the US Great Plains during the three growing seasons (2011 to 2013). CH<sub>4</sub> flux and gas diffusivity were measured using a chamber headspace method in combination with a tracer gas. At each CH<sub>4</sub> flux and gas diffusivity measurement, six chambers were used at each plot. Net methanotroph activity was calculated using net CH<sub>4</sub> uptake and gas diffusivity rates. Environmental factors that could affect CH<sub>4</sub> fluxes were measured, including soil water content, soil temperature and soil pH. Static soil properties were also measured, including bulk density, soil texture, and carbon and nitrogen content and their stable isotope ratios.

Nine Mile (NM), Nebraska, tall grass
Ordway (OR), South Dakota, tall and short grass mix
Shortgrass Steppe (SG), Colorado, short grass
Sand Hill (SH), Nebraska, tall and short grass mix
Sevilleta (SV), New Mexico, desert
